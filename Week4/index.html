Mississippi Roast

This is a delicious and easy crock pot recipe for busy moms. You only need a few ingredients. Once it is in the crock pot you can forget about it and play ith your kids!!!

Ingredients

  • ChuckRoast
  • Pepperoncini Peppers
  • 1/2 Stick Butter
  • 1/3 Cup of Mayo
  • 1 Table Spoon of Apple Cider Vinegar
  • Sprinkle of Paprika and Dill

Instruction

  1. Salt and Pepper your meat
  2. Cover with a little flour
  3. Add a little oil to your cast iron skillet
  4. Seer your meat until caramelized
  5. Add to crock pot
  6. In a small bowl mix together mayo, apple cider, paprika, and dill
  7. Smear this mayo mixture all over the roast
  8. Add butter and 5 pepperoncinis to crock pot
  9. Low for 8 hours or High for 4
